COCOA COOKIE RECIPE

Indulge in the perfect fusion of flavors with our Strawberry Cocoa Cookies topped with White Chocolate Chips. These cookies are a delightful treat that combines the rich, velvety taste of cocoa with the sweet and slightly tangy flavor of fresh strawberries, creating a mouthwatering harmony that will leave you craving more.

Each cookie is lovingly baked to achieve a soft and chewy texture that melts in your mouth, with bursts of juicy strawberry pieces in every bite. The addition of premium cocoa powder enhances the deep chocolate flavor, making these cookies a dream come true for chocolate lovers.

To elevate the experience, we’ve generously topped each cookie with creamy white chocolate chips. The white chocolate adds a smooth, sweet contrast to the intense cocoa, while its creamy texture complements the chewiness of the cookie. The result is a decadent and visually appealing treat that's perfect for any occasion.

Whether you’re a fan of classic chocolate cookies or looking to try something new, these cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and bring a touch of elegance to your snacking experience. Treat yourself to the delightful combination of strawberries, cocoa, and white chocolate, and savor the deliciousness in every bite.

Ingredients

For the Cookies:

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powderr
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Intructions

1. Prepare the Dough:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar until creamy and smooth. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips if using.

2. Shape and Bake:

  • Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are still slightly soft.
  • Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

3. Final Touch:

  • Once cooled, enjoy your delicious cocoa cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite beverage.

Tips

  • For an extra chocolatey treat, drizzle melted chocolate over the cooled cookies.
  • Store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.
  • For a festive touch, roll the dough balls in sugar before baking.

And your delicious Strawberry Cocoa Cookie is ready!
