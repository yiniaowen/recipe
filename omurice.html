OMU-RICE RECIPE

Experience a comforting classic with our Chinese Egg Tomato Rice, a simple yet profoundly satisfying dish that combines the sweet tang of ripe tomatoes with the savory richness of scrambled eggs, all served over a bed of fluffy rice.

The dish begins with the vibrant, sun-kissed flavors of juicy tomatoes, simmered to perfection until they release their natural sweetness and form a luscious, slightly tangy sauce. This tomato base is then generously mixed with light, fluffy scrambled eggs, which add a creamy texture and a rich, savory depth. The eggs are seasoned to perfection, ensuring each bite is a harmonious blend of flavors.

Served over a steaming bowl of tender, white rice, this dish offers a delightful contrast between the soft, aromatic rice and the flavorful tomato-egg mixture. The rice absorbs the delicious sauce, making every spoonful a burst of comforting flavor.

Garnished with a sprinkle of fresh chopped scallions and a dash of white pepper, our Chinese Egg Tomato Rice is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight. Ingredients

For the Egg Tomato Mixture:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, sliced

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth

Intructions

1. Prepare the Egg Tomato Mixture:

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  • Add the minced garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they start to soften, about 5 minutes.
  • In a bowl, beat the eggs and pour them into the skillet with the tomatoes. Stir gently to scramble and cook until the eggs are just set.
  • Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), sugar, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  • Garnish with sliced green onions.

2. Prepare the Rice:

  • Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a rice cooker or pot, combine the rice and water or chicken broth. Cook according to the rice cookerâ€™s instructions or on the stovetop until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

3. Assemble the Dish:

  • Rice Variations: Feel free to use brown rice or quinoa for a different twist.
  • Vegetable Add-ins: Add bell peppers, spinach, or mushrooms for extra nutrition.
  • Make Ahead: The egg tomato mixture can be made ahead and reheated for a quick meal.

Tips

  • For an extra boost of matcha flavor, you can mix a little matcha powder into the cake batter and frosting.
  • Make sure the butter and eggs are at room temperature for the best texture and consistency.
  • Store any leftover cake in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

And your delicious Omu-Rice is ready!
