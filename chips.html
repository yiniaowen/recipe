JUICY CHICKEN BURGER RECIPE

Savor the succulent goodness of our Chicken Burger, a mouthwatering delight that combines a perfectly seasoned, juicy chicken patty with a medley of fresh, flavorful toppings. Each bite starts with a tender, grilled chicken breast, marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and herbs that create a savory, irresistible flavor. The patty is grilled to perfection, ensuring a crispy, golden exterior while maintaining a moist and flavorful interior.

Nestled in a soft, toasted brioche bun, the chicken patty is accompanied by crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, and a layer of tangy pickles that add a refreshing crunch. The burger is generously topped with a creamy, homemade mayo or a zesty barbecue sauce, which enhances the flavors and adds a smooth, satisfying finish.

For an extra touch of indulgence, add a slice of melted cheddar cheese or a savory bacon strip, which melts into the patty, creating a rich, savory experience. The combination of textures and flavors—from the tender chicken and fresh veggies to the soft bun and creamy sauce—creates a harmonious and delightful taste sensation.

Perfect for a quick lunch or a casual dinner, our Chicken Burger is a gourmet treat that promises to satisfy your cravings and leave you coming back for more. Enjoy this classic favorite with a side of crispy fries or a refreshing salad for a meal that’s both satisfying and delicious.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Patties:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Burger Assembly:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Intructions

1. Prepare the Chicken Patties:

  • In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  • Shape the mixture into 4 patties, ensuring they are even in thickness for consistent cooking.
  • Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

2. Prepare the Frosting:

  • In a large bowl, beat the butter until creamy using an electric mixer on medium speed.
  • Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating well after each addition. Add the matcha powder and beat until combined.
  • Add 2 tablespoons of heavy cream (or milk) and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and spreadable. If the frosting is too thick, add more cream a tablespoon at a time until the desired consistency is reached.

3. Assemble the Burgers:

  • Toast the hamburger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  • Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun.
  • Place a chicken patty on each bun, then layer with lettuce, tomato slices, red onion slices, and pickles.
  • Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the buns and place them on top of the assembled ingredients.

4. Serve and Enjoy:

  • Serve the chicken burgers immediately with your favorite side dishes, such as fries or a salad.
  • Enjoy your homemade, juicy chicken burger!

Tips

  • For added flavor, try mixing in your favorite herbs and spices into the chicken patty mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier burger, add some chili flakes or hot sauce to the patties.
  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure your patties are cooked to a safe temperature.

And your delicious Juicy Chicken Burger is ready!
