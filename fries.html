CRINKLE FRIES RECIPE

Dive into a world of crispy, golden perfection with our irresistible Crinkle Fries. Each fry is expertly cut to create those signature ridges that offer the perfect balance of crunch and fluffiness in every bite.

These crinkle-cut wonders are meticulously fried to achieve a mouthwatering crisp exterior, while the inside remains tender and light. The unique shape not only makes them visually appealing but also ensures they capture just the right amount of seasoning, delivering a burst of savory flavor with each bite.

Ideal as a side dish, snack, or the ultimate comfort food indulgence, our Crinkle Fries are versatile enough to pair with anything from burgers and hotdogs to gourmet sandwiches and hearty salads. Whether you're dipping them in ketchup, smothering them in cheese, or enjoying them plain, these fries are sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Savor the crunch, relish the flavor, and enjoy the simple yet unforgettable pleasure of our Crinkle Fries!

Ingredients

For the Fries:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Intructions

1. Prepare the Potatoes:

  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into crinkle shapes using a crinkle cutter.
  • Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.

2. Season and Bake:

  • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a paper towel. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with vegetable oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  • Spread the seasoned potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.

3. Serve:

  • Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips

  • For extra crispy fries, after soaking, blanch the cut potatoes in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then proceed with the recipe.
  • Try adding different seasonings like cayenne pepper, onion powder, or dried herbs for a unique twist.

And your crunchy Crinkle Fries are ready!
