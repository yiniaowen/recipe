CEASER SALAD RECIPE

Experience the perfect blend of crisp and creamy with our Caesar Salad—a timeless classic that never goes out of style. This salad starts with a bed of fresh, crisp romaine lettuce, known for its crunchy texture and vibrant flavor. Each leaf is generously tossed with a rich and tangy Caesar dressing, made from scratch with a delightful mix of garlic, anchovies, Dijon mustard, and the unmistakable tang of freshly squeezed lemon juice.

The salad is further elevated with a generous sprinkling of freshly grated Parmesan cheese, which adds a sharp and nutty bite. Toasted croutons are scattered throughout, offering a satisfying crunch that contrasts beautifully with the creamy dressing. Every bite delivers a harmonious blend of savory, tangy, and umami flavors, making this Caesar Salad a standout dish that's both luxurious and comforting.

Perfect as a refreshing appetizer or a light, satisfying main course, our Caesar Salad is sure to delight with its balanced flavors and hearty texture. For an added touch of indulgence, consider topping it with grilled chicken or crispy bacon. Enjoy a taste of culinary perfection with each forkful!

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 large head of Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 boiled eggs

For the Dressing:

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Intructions

1. Prepare the Dressing:

  • In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and Parmesan cheese.
  • Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously to emulsify the dressing.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the consistency by adding more olive oil if needed.

2. Assemble the Salad:

  • In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped Romaine lettuce and croutons.
  • Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss until the lettuce is well coated.
  • Sprinkle with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, you can add grilled chicken or bacon to the salad.
  • Make sure the lettuce is dry before adding the dressing to prevent it from becoming soggy.

And your delicious Healthy Ceaser Salad is ready!
