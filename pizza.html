HOME-MADE PIZZA RECIPE

Indulge in the mouthwatering delight of our homemade pizza, crafted with love and the freshest ingredients. This pizza features a perfectly crispy crust that's light and airy on the inside, topped with a rich, tangy tomato sauce that bursts with the flavors of sun-ripened tomatoes, garlic, and a hint of basil.

Generously sprinkled with a blend of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, each bite delivers a gooey, cheesy goodness that stretches with every pull. But it doesn't stop there – we've piled on your favorite toppings, from vibrant bell peppers and earthy mushrooms to spicy pepperoni and savory sausage, all roasted to perfection.

The final touch? A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of fresh basil leaves, adding a fragrant, herbaceous note that ties all the flavors together. Whether you enjoy it fresh out of the oven or savor it as a leftover snack, this homemade pizza promises a symphony of tastes and textures that will make your taste buds sing. Perfect for family dinners, friendly gatherings, or a cozy night in, our homemade pizza is more than just a meal – it's an experience.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • ½ cup sliced olives
  • Fresh basil leaves.

Intructions

1. Prepare the Dough:

  • In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  • Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until the dough forms.
  • Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

2. Prepare the Sauce:

  • In a medium bowl, mix tomato sauce, tomato paste, olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Stir until well combined.

3. Assemble the Pizza:

  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal parts.
  • Roll out each dough ball on a floured surface to your desired thickness..
  • Transfer the rolled dough to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  • Spread a layer of sauce over the dough.
  • Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • Add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, bell peppers, and olives.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

4. Serve:

  • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  • Slice and serve hot.

Tips

  • For a crispier crust, bake the pizza on a preheated pizza stone.
  • Experiment with different toppings to create your own pizza masterpiece.
  • Store leftover pizza in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

And your delicious Home-made Pizza is ready!
