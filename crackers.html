BERRY CRACKER RECIPE

Indulge in the delightful combination of creamy, sweet, and tangy flavors with our Berry Cream Crackers. This exquisite treat features a perfect balance of crispy, buttery crackers, rich cream cheese, and a medley of fresh, juicy berries. Each bite is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it an irresistible snack or dessert for any occasion.

Our Berry Cream Crackers are not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight. The vibrant colors of the fresh berries – strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries – contrast beautifully with the creamy white cheese and golden-brown crackers, creating a stunning presentation that will impress your guests.

The base of this delectable treat is a selection of premium crackers, chosen for their perfect crunch and subtle flavor, which complement the richness of the cream cheese. The cream cheese is whipped to perfection, light and airy, with just the right amount of sweetness to enhance the natural flavors of the berries.

Perfect for afternoon tea, brunch, or a light dessert, our Berry Cream Crackers are versatile and easy to serve. They are a fantastic option for entertaining, as they can be prepared in advance and assembled quickly when needed. Enjoy the refreshing and satisfying taste of our Berry Cream Crackers and make any moment special.

Ingredients

For the Crackers:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • ¼ cup cold water

For the Berry Cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

For Garnish:

  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Powdered sugar
  • Extra berries

Intructions

1. Prepare the Crackers:

  • Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and sugar.
  • Cut in Butter: Add the cubed butter to the flour mixture. Use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  • Add Water: Gradually add the cold water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together.
  • Roll Out Dough: On a floured surface, roll out the dough to a ⅛-inch thickness. Cut into desired cracker shapes.
  • Bake: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the crackers on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Let cool completely.

    • 2. Prepare the Berry Cream:

    • Whip Cream: In a large bowl, beat the heavy cream with an electric mixer on medium speed until soft peaks form.
    • Add Sugar and Vanilla: Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Continue beating until stiff peaks form.
    • Fold in Berries: Gently fold in the mixed berries.

    3. Assemble the Berry Cream Crackers:

    • Layer Cream on Crackers: Spread a generous dollop of berry cream on each cracker.
    • Garnish: Top with fresh mint leaves and extra berries for garnish.

    4. Serve:

    • Presentation: Arrange the berry cream crackers on a serving platter.
    • Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing combination of creamy berries and crispy crackers.

Tips

  • Make Ahead: Crackers can be baked ahead of time and stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
  • Berry Mix: Feel free to use any combination of berries you like or have on hand.
  • Chilled Cream: For best results, chill the bowl and beaters before whipping the cream.

And your delicious Strawberry Cocoa Cookie is ready!
