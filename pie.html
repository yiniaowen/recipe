STRAWBERRY PIE RECIPE

Dive into a slice of pure delight with our Strawberry-Topped Pie. This decadent dessert begins with a golden, buttery crust that is perfectly flaky and tender, providing the ideal foundation for the luscious layers above.

Inside, you’ll find a rich, velvety filling that melts in your mouth, offering a symphony of flavors with each bite. The creamy custard base is infused with just the right amount of sweetness, creating a smooth and dreamy texture that contrasts beautifully with the crisp crust.

Topping this masterpiece are fresh, vibrant strawberries, handpicked for their exceptional flavor and juiciness. Each berry is carefully arranged in a beautiful, eye-catching pattern, their bright red hue inviting you to indulge. The strawberries are glazed to perfection, adding a glossy finish that enhances their natural sweetness and gives the pie a stunning, irresistible shine.

The combination of the buttery crust, creamy filling, and succulent strawberries creates a harmonious blend of textures and flavors that will captivate your taste buds. Whether served at a summer picnic, a family gathering, or a special occasion, our Strawberry-Topped Pie is the ultimate treat that promises to delight and impress with every delicious bite.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Topping:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • ¼ cup strawberry jam

Intructions

1. Prepare the Crust:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and sugar. Add melted butter and mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
  • Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
  • Bake for 8-10 minutes, then let it cool completely.

2. Prepare the Filling:

  • In a large bowl, beat the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Set aside.
  • In another bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract, beating until well combined.
  • Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until smooth and well incorporated.

3. Assemble the Pie:

  • Spread the cream filling evenly over the cooled crust.
  • Arrange the sliced strawberries on top of the filling in a circular pattern.

4. Add the Topping:

  • In a small saucepan, warm the strawberry jam over low heat until it becomes slightly runny.
  • Brush the jam over the strawberries to give them a glossy finish.

5. Chill and Serve:

  • Refrigerate the pie for at least 2 hours before serving to allow it to set.
  • Slice and enjoy the refreshing, creamy pie topped with fresh strawberries!

Tips

  • For a firmer crust, chill the crust for 30 minutes before adding the filling.
  • Use the freshest strawberries you can find for the best flavor and appearance.
  • Store any leftover pie in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

And your delicious Matcha Cake is ready!
