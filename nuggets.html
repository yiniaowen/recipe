CHEESY POTATO NUGGETS RECIPE

Discover the ultimate comfort food with our irresistibly delicious Potato Nuggets. These bite-sized delights are a perfect blend of creamy mashed potatoes, savory seasonings, and a crispy, golden exterior that delivers a satisfying crunch with every bite.

Each nugget starts with perfectly cooked, fluffy potatoes that are mashed to a smooth, velvety texture. We then mix in a blend of flavorful spices, including garlic, onion, and a hint of paprika, to give the nuggets a savory depth that tantalizes your taste buds. The mashed potato mixture is shaped into bite-sized nuggets and coated in a light, crispy breadcrumb crust, ensuring every bite is an explosion of texture and flavor.

Ingredients

For the Nuggets:

  • 3 large potatoes, peeled and boiled
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Â½ cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Oil for frying

Intructions

1. Prepare the Potato Nuggets:

  • In a large bowl, mash the boiled potatoes until smooth.
  • Add breadcrumbs, grated cheese, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano to the mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  • Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into nugget-sized pieces.
  • Dip each nugget into the beaten egg, ensuring it is well-coated.
  • Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the nuggets in batches until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

2. Serve:

  • Arrange the potato nuggets on a serving plate.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley or chives if desired.
  • Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ketchup, mayonnaise, or a spicy aioli.

  • Add some chopped veggies like bell peppers or corn to the potato mixture for extra flavor and nutrition.
