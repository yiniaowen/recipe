SHRIMP ALFREDO PASTA RECIPE

Experience a luxurious meal with our Shrimp Alfredo Pasta, where creamy, velvety Alfredo sauce meets succulent, perfectly cooked shrimp. This dish combines the rich, buttery flavors of classic Alfredo sauce with the delicate sweetness of plump shrimp, creating a truly indulgent dining experience.

The Alfredo sauce is made from scratch with a blend of creamy heavy cream, freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and a hint of garlic, resulting in a smooth and luscious sauce that coats each strand of pasta. The sauce is seasoned to perfection with a touch of black pepper and a hint of nutmeg, adding depth and complexity to every bite.

Tender shrimp are sautéed to golden perfection, infused with a touch of lemon juice and a sprinkle of fresh herbs, which complement the creamy Alfredo sauce beautifully. The shrimp are cooked just right—juicy and flavorful, adding a satisfying contrast to the rich pasta.

Served over a bed of al dente fettuccine, this dish is garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a generous shaving of Parmesan cheese, adding a touch of color and an extra layer of flavor. Each forkful delivers a delightful combination of creamy, savory sauce, perfectly seasoned shrimp, and tender pasta, making it a decadent choice for any special occasion or a comforting weeknight dinner.

Our Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is not just a meal; it's an experience—one that will leave you craving its rich, comforting flavors long after the last bite.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Intructions

1. Prepare the Pasta:

  • Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

2. Prepare the Alfredo Sauce:

  • In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  • Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Combine and Serve:

  • Add the cooked fettuccine to the Alfredo sauce and toss to coat.
  • Return the shrimp to the skillet and stir to combine.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and additional Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately.

Tips

  • For Extra Flavor: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.
  • The pasta and sauce can be prepared ahead of time and reheated just before serving.
  • Top with extra Parmesan, a squeeze of lemon juice, or a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

And your delicious Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is ready!
