CLASSRIC CHICKEN RAMEN RECIPE

Dive into the heartwarming bowl of Classic Chicken Ramen, where rich flavors and comforting textures come together to create a truly satisfying experience. This traditional Japanese dish features a savory, aromatic broth that simmers with tender chicken, fresh vegetables, and a medley of spices, creating a harmonious symphony of taste in every spoonful.

The broth, the soul of the ramen, is carefully crafted to achieve a deep, umami-rich flavor. It’s simmered for hours with chicken bones, ginger, garlic, and scallions, resulting in a velvety, golden liquid that’s both nourishing and indulgent. Each sip reveals layers of flavor, from the subtle sweetness of the caramelized onions to the gentle heat of the white pepper.

Nestled in this flavorful broth are perfectly cooked ramen noodles, with a delightful chew and a slightly wavy texture that captures the essence of the soup. The noodles are topped with succulent pieces of tender chicken, marinated and cooked to perfection, offering a satisfying bite that complements the noodles and broth.

Enjoy this Classic Chicken Ramen as a nourishing bowl of warmth that promises to soothe your soul and satisfy your cravings with every delicious bite.

Ingredients

For the Broth:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 inch piece of ginger (sliced)

For the Ramen:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (for garnish)

For Toppings:

  • Sliced nori (seaweed)
  • Sriracha or chili paste
  • Pickled bamboo shoots

Intructions

1. Prepare the Broth:

  • Simmer Ingredients: In a large pot, combine chicken broth, water, soy sauce, miso paste, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  • Poach Chicken: Add chicken breasts to the simmering broth. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through. Remove the chicken, shred it, and set aside.

2. Prepare the Noodles and Vegetables:

  • Cook Noodles: In a separate pot, cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Sauté Vegetables: In a skillet, sauté mushrooms and carrots until tender. Add to the broth.

3. Assemble the Ramen:

  • Divide cooked noodles between serving bowls. Ladle the hot broth and vegetables over the noodles. Top with shredded chicken, spinach, and soft-boiled eggs.

4. Garnish and Serve:

  • Garnish with green onions, sesame seeds, and any optional toppings. Serve immediately.

Tips

  • To make perfect soft-boiled eggs, cook them for 6-7 minutes, then transfer to ice water to cool before peeling.
  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables or proteins to make this ramen your own.

And your delicious Classic Chicken Ramen is ready!
