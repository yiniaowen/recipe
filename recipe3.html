CHOCOLATE MOUSSE CAKE RECIPE

Experience pure indulgence with our Chocolate Mousse Cake, a dessert that epitomizes luxury and decadence. This cake features a rich, velvety chocolate mousse encased in a delicate, chocolate sponge base. Each bite reveals a sumptuous blend of creamy, bittersweet chocolate that melts effortlessly on your tongue, delivering a deep, satisfying flavor that chocolate lovers crave.

The mousse itself is crafted with the finest cocoa, creating a texture that's both airy and luscious. It’s lovingly folded with whipped cream to achieve a light, yet decadently creamy consistency, making each mouthful a blissful experience.

Topping this divine creation is a glossy, ripe cherry that adds a burst of vibrant color and a hint of fruity sweetness. The cherry, with its slightly tart flavor, perfectly contrasts the richness of the chocolate mousse, offering a refreshing and delightful twist. It’s delicately nestled on a bed of rich chocolate ganache that adds an extra layer of indulgence.

Finished with a drizzle of dark chocolate and a sprinkle of edible gold flakes, this cake is a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Whether served as the centerpiece of a celebration or enjoyed as a special treat, our Chocolate Mousse Cake topped with cherry promises to elevate any occasion with its elegance and exquisite flavor.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Mousse:

  • 8 oz (225 g) semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Topping:

  • 1 cup fresh cherries, pitted
  • ¼ cup cherry preserves
  • Optional: whipped cream for garnish

Intructions

1. Prepare the Cake:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round cake pans.
  • In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Stir in boiling water and mix until smooth. Add oil, eggs, and vanilla; mix until well combined.
  • Divide batter between the prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

2. Prepare the Mousse:

  • Melt the chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water. Let it cool slightly.
  • In a saucepan, heat ½ cup of the cream until just boiling. Whisk in the egg yolks and sugar, and cook over low heat until thickened.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla extract. Let cool to room temperature.
  • Whip the remaining cream to soft peaks. Gently fold it into the chocolate mixture until well combined.

3. Assemble the Cake:

  • Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of mousse over the top.
  • Add the second cake layer on top of the mousse and spread mousse over the top and sides of the cake
  • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

4. Prepare the Topping:

  • Heat the cherry preserves until melted and glossy. Brush over the top of the cake.
  • Arrange fresh cherries on top of the cake. Optionally, garnish with whipped cream.

Tips

  • For a smoother mousse, make sure to let the chocolate cool slightly before folding in the whipped cream.
  • Garnish with mint leaves for a fresh touch.
  • Keep the cake refrigerated until ready to serve to maintain its texture and freshness.

And your delicious Chocolate Mousse Cake is ready!
