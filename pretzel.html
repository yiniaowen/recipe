HEART SHAPED DONUT RECIPE

Indulge in the sweet romance of our Heart-Shaped Donuts, where love and flavor come together in a delightful pastry. Each donut is carefully crafted into a perfect heart shape, symbolizing affection and joy with every bite.

Our heart-shaped donuts boast a golden-brown exterior that's lightly crisp, giving way to a soft, fluffy interior that melts in your mouth. These donuts are generously coated in a glistening glaze that adds just the right amount of sweetness, ensuring a delectable treat that is irresistible from the first taste to the last crumb.

To make these donuts even more special, we've added a touch of whimsy with vibrant sprinkles or a delicate drizzle of chocolate, adding an extra layer of indulgence. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, expressing your love, or simply treating yourself to something sweet, our heart-shaped donuts are the perfect way to make any moment more delightful.

Perfect for Valentine's Day, anniversaries, or any time you want to share a little love, these heart-shaped donuts are more than just a dessert; they're a heartfelt gesture that brings a smile with every bite.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Red or pink food coloring (optional)

Intructions

1. Prepare the Donuts:

  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix the milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract..
  • Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.

2. Shape the Donuts:

  • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/2 inch thickness..
  • Use a heart-shaped cookie cutter to cut out the donuts.

3. Fry the Donuts:

  • Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  • Fry the donuts a few at a time until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  • Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

4. Glaze the Donuts:

  • In a bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make the glaze.
  • Add food coloring if desired.
  • Dip the cooled donuts into the glaze and add sprinkles.

And your delicious Donut is ready!
